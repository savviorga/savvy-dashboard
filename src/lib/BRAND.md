# Savvy — Brand guidelines

## Concept
- **Name:** savvy (lowercase)
- **Meaning:** Smart, calm, intelligent money management
- **Style:** Minimalist, premium, modern, elegant
- **Target:** Users who want clarity and control over personal finances without stress

## Visual identity

### Color palette
| Role | Light | Dark | Usage |
|------|--------|------|--------|
| Primary | `#0E0E11` | `#fafafa` | Text, key UI |
| Background | `#F8F9FA` | `#0E0E11` | Page background |
| Elevated | `#ffffff` | `#18181b` | Cards, header, sidebar |
| Accent | `#1DB954` | `#22c55e` | CTAs, positive numbers, active states (sparingly) |
| Gray 900 | `#2A2A2E` | `#a1a1aa` | Secondary text |
| Gray 300 | `#D1D5DB` | `#3f3f46` | Borders, dividers |
| Muted | `#71717a` | `#71717a` | Hints, labels |

### Typography
- **Font:** Inter (weights 400, 500, 600). SF Pro on Apple when available.
- **Weights:** Prefer medium (500) and semibold (600). No heavy bold.
- **Logo:** Wordmark "savvy" in lowercase, semibold, tracking-tight.

### UI
- **Radius:** 16px cards (`--savvy-radius`), 12px small elements (`--savvy-radius-sm`).
- **White space:** Generous padding; avoid clutter.
- **Motion:** Subtle only (fade-in, 200–500ms transitions).
- **Dark mode:** Mandatory; same structure, premium feel.

### Logo
- Wordmark: lowercase "savvy".
- Optional icon: stylized upward line (growth), no coins/piggy banks.

## Tone of voice
- Calm, intelligent, supportive. Never judgmental.
- Example: instead of "You're overspending" → "Your expenses increased 12% this month. Want to review them?"

## Tagline options
- Claridad y control sobre tu dinero
- Tu dinero, con claridad
- Finanzas personales sin estrés
